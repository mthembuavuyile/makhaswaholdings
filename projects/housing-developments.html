Residential Housing Developments

Project Overview

Makhaswa Holdings delivers large-scale housing developments designed to meet the growing need for high-quality, durable residential units across Gauteng and South Africa. As an NHBRC-registered general building contractor, we carry out full-service developments from greenfield site layouts through to completed handovers of residential estates and housing projects.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our building teams manage all phases of residential construction. This includes laying out foundations, structural brickwork, roof installations, mechanical and electrical engineering systems (HVAC, power supply), plumbing installations, and internal plastering and finishes. Our CIDB Grade 6GB capability enables us to handle multi-unit developments and commercial building complexes seamlessly.

Compliance is at the core of our operations. Every unit is constructed to conform strictly to the National Home Builders Registration Council (NHBRC) standards and SANS 10400 building guidelines, ensuring structural safety, energy efficiency, and long-term durability for families and residents.

Social Impact & Quality Delivery

In addition to physical construction, Makhaswa Holdings is committed to socio-economic development. During housing construction, we recruit local labor, run on-site training workshops, and support local sub-contractors, empowering Gauteng communities. Our projects are executed under ISO 9001 and ISO 45001 frameworks, delivering safe sites and exceptional quality.
