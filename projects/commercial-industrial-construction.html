Commercial & Industrial Construction

Project Overview

Makhaswa Holdings builds structural facilities that support economic and business operations. Operating under our Grade 6GB PE (General Building), 8ME PE (Mechanical Engineering), and 7EB PE (Electrical Engineering) gradings, we execute construction projects for commercial offices, industrial warehouses, factories, and storage centers across Pretoria, Midrand, and Gauteng.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our commercial building division manages the entire execution lifecycle. This includes sub-surface geotechnical prep, reinforced concrete foundation slabs, structural steel framing, brickwork, roofing installations, fire containment systems, and heavy-duty industrial flooring. We coordinate the seamless installation of HVAC systems, electrical distribution grids, and commercial plumbing lines.

Every commercial build conforms to NHBRC, municipal building regulations, and SANS 10400 safety specifications. We deploy dedicated project managers on-site to handle sub-contractors, monitor schedules, optimize material workflows, and guarantee high-quality finishes and structural compliance.

Delivery Metrics & Transformations

Industrial developments require strict compliance with safety, environmental, and engineering codes. Makhaswa Holdings operates under a SHEQ management system. With Level 1 B-BBEE recognition, we maximize local procurement, support local suppliers, and train local community builders, ensuring our developments contribute to sustainable community growth.
