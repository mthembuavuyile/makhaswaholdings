Engineering Project Delivery

Project Overview

Makhaswa Holdings offers comprehensive engineering project delivery services, overseeing construction and civil infrastructure projects from initial plans to final handover. Operating under our CIDB Grade 8CE PE, 8ME PE, and 7EB PE capabilities, we provide professional site supervision, precise land surveying, project coordination, and technical planning to ensure projects are executed safely, on time, and to exact engineering design specifications.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our project delivery team manages on-site coordination, technical inspections, scheduling, resource management, and risk mitigation. Using advanced surveying equipment and digital BIM coordination, we verify subgrade preparations, road alignments, concrete reinforcement codes, and structural integrity, coordinating directly with architects, engineers, and sub-contractors.

Quality Assurance & Compliance

We manage projects under strict ISO 9001 (Quality), ISO 14001 (Environmental), and ISO 45001 (Safety) standards, ensuring safe work environments and high-precision execution. Our team of certified project managers and surveyors operates with absolute transparency, efficiency, and compliance to meet public and private sector tender requirements.
