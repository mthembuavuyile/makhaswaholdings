Building & Housing Developments

Project Overview

Makhaswa Holdings builds structural facilities that support economic operations and delivers large-scale housing developments designed to meet the growing need for high-quality, durable units across South Africa. Operating under our Grade 6GB PE (General Building), 8ME PE (Mechanical Engineering), and 7EB PE (Electrical Engineering) gradings and NHBRC registration, we execute construction projects for commercial offices, warehouses, factories, schools, clinics, and residential estates.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our building division manages the entire execution lifecycle. This includes sub-surface geotechnical prep, reinforced concrete foundation slabs, structural steel framing, brickwork, roofing installations, fire containment, and internal plastering and finishes. Every build conforms to NHBRC, municipal building regulations, and SANS 10400 safety specifications, ensuring structural safety, energy efficiency, and long-term durability.

Delivery Metrics & Impact

Industrial and residential developments require strict compliance with safety, environmental, and engineering codes. Makhaswa Holdings operates under ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 frameworks. With Level 1 B-BBEE recognition, we maximize local procurement, support local suppliers, and train local community builders, ensuring our developments contribute to sustainable community growth.
