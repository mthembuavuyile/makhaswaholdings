Road Construction & Surfacing

Project Overview

Makhaswa Holdings delivers multi-disciplinary road building, resurfacing, and pavement engineering. Backed by our Grade 8CE PE grading and our extensive internal machinery fleet, our road construction division implements projects ranging from municipal road rehabilitation to heavy industrial surfacing and paving across Gauteng and surrounding provinces.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our scope of road construction includes earth profiling, sub-grade preparation, layer works (G1 to G7 material layout), concrete soil stabilization, and asphalt overlays/inlays. We manage the installation of road drainage elements including pre-cast concrete kerbing, storm water channelling, stone pitching, guardrail installations, and commercial interlocking paving solutions.

We leverage our internal fleet of graders, TLBs, compactors, and asphalt paving equipment. By avoiding reliance on third-party machinery hires, we control project scheduling tightly and guarantee compliance with Committee of Land Transport Officials (COLTO) and SANRAL standards on road levels, compaction density, and drainage fall.

Community Impact & Quality Assurance

Road infrastructure connects communities and improves safety. Makhaswa Holdings prioritizes road safety during construction with professional traffic diversion systems and warning layouts. We utilize local labor for sub-contracting paving, stone pitching, and kerbing scopes, ensuring local community empowerment on every road contract. Our processes are fully ISO 9001 and ISO 45001 certified.
