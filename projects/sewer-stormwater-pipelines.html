Project Overview

Reliable water management is critical for modern infrastructure. Makhaswa Holdings carries out civil engineering works for installing sewer reticulation systems and stormwater drainage networks. Operating under our Grade 8CE PE status, we support municipalities, housing developments, and commercial developers by building robust sub-surface utility networks in Pretoria, Johannesburg, and across Gauteng.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our scope of pipe installation covers trench excavation, shoring, pipe laying (HDPE, PVC, and pre-cast concrete stormwater pipes), joint sealing, manhole construction, and backfilling. We construct stormwater drainage channels, detention ponds, stone pitching, gabions, and Reno mattresses to manage water flow, prevent soil erosion, and secure pipeline crossings.

We ensure that all gravity lines are surveyed and profiled to exact fall requirements to prevent flow blockage. Pipeline networks are pressure tested, and stormwater systems are verified to handle peak water volumes in compliance with SANS 1200 specifications and municipal utility guidelines.

Safety & Municipal Compliance

Deep trenching requires strict safety compliance to protect personnel. Makhaswa Holdings employs standard shoring methodologies and safety boundaries during excavation. We are ISO 9001 and ISO 45001 accredited, assuring municipal engineers and clients that all utility infrastructure is constructed safely, durable, and to exact project specifications.
