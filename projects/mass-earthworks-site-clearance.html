Earthworks & Site Prep

Project Overview

Before any structure can be built, the site must be cleared and graded with precision. Makhaswa Holdings delivers bulk earthworks, site clearance, and demolition services that prepare land for large-scale developments. Using our CIDB Grade 8CE PE capability, we execute earthmoving projects for highway alignments, housing developments, and commercial complexes across Gauteng.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our earthworks scope includes site de-vegetation, boulder removal, controlled demolition of existing structures, cut-and-fill balancing, mass excavation, and subgrade compaction. We perform geological site prep, ensuring unstable soils are excavated and replaced with engineered fill materials to prevent future foundation shifting or settlement.

We deploy our own fleet of earthmoving plant, including heavy excavators, dump trucks, grader machinery, smooth-drum rollers, and TLBs. This internal fleet allows us to maintain strict timeline controls and deliver site preparations that meet rigorous engineering compaction codes (tested to Mod AASHTO densities).

Environmental & Safety Compliance

Large-scale earthmoving is highly regulated in South Africa. Makhaswa Holdings operates in compliance with environmental regulations, ensuring proper disposal of building rubble, silt control during rainy periods, and minimal dust pollution. Safety is prioritized on our sites with active machine zoning and barricades, backed by ISO 14001 and ISO 45001 certifications.
