Concrete Foundations & Structures

Project Overview

Makhaswa Holdings offers expert civil engineering capabilities for pouring heavy concrete foundations and building complex structural concrete layouts. Working under our Grade 8CE PE grading, our engineering division handles structural concrete projects for commercial buildings, warehouse slabs, retaining structures, and industrial developments in Pretoria and across Gauteng.

Key Scope & Civil Solutions

Our scope of work covers steel reinforcement (rebar profiling and fixing), shuttering, formwork assembly, concrete mix optimization, and precision pouring. We utilize state-of-the-art concrete vibrators and leveling tools to guarantee density, stability, and zero air pockets. We also specialize in custom concrete panels, tilt-up construction, retaining walls, and sub-surface footings.

Every pour undergoes strict slump testing and cube strength verification (tested at 7, 14, and 28 days) to ensure structural specifications meet SANS 1200 standards. Our teams operate with meticulous attention to civil drawings, tolerances, and concrete curing durations, ensuring load-bearing integrity for heavy structures.

Safety & Engineering Excellence

Pouring heavy structural foundations requires high levels of on-site coordination and strict safety compliance. Makhaswa Holdings executes all concrete operations under our SHEQ management system. With Level 1 B-BBEE status and ISO accreditation, we ensure all projects align with national standards and public safety requirements, delivering zero-harm construction sites.
