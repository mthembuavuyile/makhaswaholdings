Who We Are

Makhaswa Holdings (Pty) Ltd is a fully black-owned civil engineering and construction company headquartered in Gauteng, South Africa, with operational offices in Midrand and Pretoria. Officially registered on 17 July 2017, the company has grown into a reputable multi-disciplinary contractor trusted by both public and private sector clients for the successful delivery of infrastructure and construction projects.

With a strong focus on quality, efficiency, and reliability, Makhaswa Holdings consistently delivers projects on time and within budget while contributing to sustainable community development.

"We don't just construct buildings — we create lasting legacies. From concept to completion, we bring your vision to life with precision, passion, and excellence."

Our Journey

Established in 2017, Makhaswa Holdings is a private company that has grown into a reliable, multi-disciplined construction firm based in Garsfontein, Pretoria.

2017
Makhaswa Holdings founded. Entry into residential and small-scale civil works in Gauteng.
2019
Awarded first major government infrastructure contract. Expanded plant & equipment fleet.
2022
Achieved Level 1 B-BBEE status with 135% procurement recognition.
2024
